Error Analysis & Failure Cases

Understanding not just what works, but why models fail is essential. This page presents representative failure cases, systematic confusion patterns, class-specific weaknesses, and practical lessons learned from our experiments.

Figures directory: place failure case visuals and charts for this page in ../figures/error-analysis/.
Context

Error types & patterns

Categorizing and understanding the different sources of errors.

Common error categories

  • Semantic confusion: Model confuses visually or semantically similar classes that could be legitimate failures
  • Modality mismatch: Image and text point to different classes (e.g., misleading caption)
  • Out-of-distribution: Sample looks different from training data (unusual lighting, angle, style)
  • Ambiguity: Sample could reasonably belong to multiple classes (genuinely ambiguous examples)
  • Label noise: Sample is mislabeled in the ground truth
  • Feature ambiguity: Both modalities are informative but point in conflicting directions
