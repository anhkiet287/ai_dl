Cases

Failure cases

Hard cases the models miss most often.

  • Style & Beauty vs Travel vs Food & Drink share lifestyle vocabulary and are the main confusion cluster.
  • Queer Voices and Sports are minority classes; many false negatives fall into the head class (Politics).
  • Short headlines (<=10 tokens) without the description field lose context and misroute to Politics.
  • Unseen named entities (cities, public figures) lower confidence across models.

Takeaways

Lessons learned

Actionable fixes to reduce recurring errors.

  • Add class weighting or focal loss to further protect tail categories.
  • Extend sequence length for Transformer (>=256) and fine-tune longer.
  • Light data cleaning: deduplicate near-identical headlines; normalize named entities.
  • Augment lifestyle/health classes (paraphrase/back-translate) to separate overlaps.
  • Calibrate thresholds per class for balanced Precision/Recall on minority labels.
