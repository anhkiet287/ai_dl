Task definition

Assignment requirements

Classify news headline + short description into one of 42 categories; report accuracy and macro-F1.

What we must show

  • End-to-end text pipeline with EDA, preprocessing, model design, and results.
  • Metrics: Accuracy and Macro-F1 on stratified 70/15/15 splits.
  • Handle class imbalance and overlapping labels (e.g., Wellness vs Healthy Living).
  • Provide figures: category distribution, text length, stopwords, TF-IDF/heatmaps.

Dataset snapshot

  • Samples: 209,527; Classes: 42.
  • Language: English headlines + short descriptions; avg length 33.5 tokens.
  • Splits: 70% train / 15% val / 15% test; stratified.
  • Max sequence length: 60 (RNNs) / 128 (DistilBERT).
Summary

Key findings

Highlights from experiments and EDA.

LSTM leads Best metrics: Acc 0.9003, Macro-F1 0.8996; BiLSTM close behind.
Transformer underperforms DistilBERT trails (Acc 0.8778, F1 0.8732) likely due to short max length and limited steps.
Imbalance + overlap Long-tail categories and similar labels drive most confusion (Wellness/Healthy Living/Parenting).
Compact text Mean length 33.5 tokens; heavy stopword ratio (~35%), impacting TF-IDF baselines.
