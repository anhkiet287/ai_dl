Project overview

What this assignment asks for

The task is classification on three data types: image, text, and multimodal. The brief requires comparison across model families: CNN vs ViT for image, RNN vs Transformer for text, and zero-shot vs few-shot for multimodal classification.

3Dataset types
5+Classes per dataset
5000+Suggested training samples for image/text

Suggested team summary

We study pretrained deep learning models on three classification settings and compare performance, robustness, and interpretability across model families.

  • Image classification with CNN and ViT
  • Text classification with RNN and Transformer
  • Multimodal classification with zero-shot and few-shot methods

Dataset checklist

  • At least 5 classes per dataset
  • Enough scale to support fair comparison
  • Real image-text pairs for multimodal data
  • Explain why each dataset was chosen
